Kamel El Adad

2020-Present • TV-14 • 3 Seasons • Comedy, Drama, Social

"Kamel El Adad" (Full House) is a popular Egyptian comedy series that follows the daily challenges and hilarious situations of an extended family living under one roof. As they navigate through life's ups and downs, the series showcases the dynamics of modern Egyptian family life with humor and heart.

Cast:

Ahmed Amin, Bayoumi Fouad, Sherif Salama, Mai Omar, Samaa Ibrahim, Sabry Fawaz

Creator:

Ahmed El Mallawany
