"Taht-Sabe Ard" (Seven Layers Below Ground) follows a team of archaeologists who make a startling discovery beneath an ancient Egyptian site - a mysterious structure that defies historical understanding and may hold supernatural secrets.

Led by renowned archaeologist Dr. Tarek Fahmy, the expedition uncovers artifacts and hieroglyphs suggesting an advanced civilization existed far earlier than previously believed. As the team ventures deeper into the underground chambers, they unleash forces they don't understand and find themselves trapped between scientific ambition and ancient warnings. The series combines archaeological mystery with psychological horror, exploring the boundaries between science, myth, and the unexplained.
