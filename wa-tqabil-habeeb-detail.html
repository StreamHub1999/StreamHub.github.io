Wa Tqabil Habeeb (وتقابل حبيب)

2024 • TV-14 • 1 Season • Romance, Drama

"Wa Tqabil Habeeb" (And You Meet a Lover) is a captivating Egyptian romantic drama that explores the complex journey of love, heartbreak, and second chances. The series follows Layla, a young woman whose life is upended when she unexpectedly crosses paths with her former love after years of separation.

Set against the vibrant backdrop of contemporary Cairo, the story weaves together themes of fate, forgiveness, and the courage to pursue happiness despite past heartaches. As old feelings resurface, both characters must navigate their complicated history while facing new challenges that test their resolve and redefine their understanding of love.

Cast:

Rogina, Ahmed Fahmy, Ayten Amer, Naglaa Badr, Mai Omar, Ahmed Magdy

Director:

Mohamed Sami
