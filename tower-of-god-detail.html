Tower of God

2020-Present ‚Ä¢ TV-14 ‚Ä¢ 2 Seasons ‚Ä¢ Anime, Action, Adventure, Fantasy

Tower of God centers around a boy called Twenty-Fifth Bam, who has spent most of his life trapped beneath a vast and mysterious Tower, with only his close friend, Rachel, to keep him company. When Rachel enters the Tower, Bam manages to open the door into it as well, and faces challenges at each floor of this tower as he tries to find his closest companion.

The Tower itself contains multiple environments, with each floor having a unique climate, geography, and flora/fauna. It hosts many different intelligent species. Living in the outer part of the Tower are residents who live in regular society, while people who "climb the tower" and face dangerous challenges on each floor, earning special status and abilities, are known as "Regulars."
