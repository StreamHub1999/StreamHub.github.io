2018 ‚Ä¢ TV-14 ‚Ä¢ 1 Season ‚Ä¢ Anime, Drama, Sci-Fi, Thriller

Steins;Gate 0 is set in an alternate worldline where Okabe Rintaro fails to save Makise Kurisu and abandons his mad scientist persona to live as an ordinary college student. Suffering from post-traumatic stress disorder, Okabe avoids time travel and the dangers it poses.

His life takes a dramatic turn when he meets Maho Hiyajo, a neuroscience researcher who worked with Kurisu, and learns about "Amadeus" - an artificial intelligence system based on Kurisu's memories. As Okabe interacts with the AI, mysterious events begin to unfold that threaten to pull him back into the world of time travel conspiracy and danger he tried to leave behind.
