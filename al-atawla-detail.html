Al Atawla (The Table)

2023-2024 • TV-14 • 2 Seasons • Crime, Drama, Thriller

"Al Atawla" (The Table) is a gripping Egyptian crime drama that follows the power struggles within the criminal underworld. The series revolves around a complex network of drug lords, corrupt officials, and ambitious players all vying for control of "The Table" - the symbolic center of power in the narcotics trade.

When a major power vacuum emerges, longtime enemies and uneasy allies must navigate betrayals, revenge plots, and shifting loyalties, all while evading law enforcement. The story interweaves personal vendettas with business conflicts, showing how the criminal hierarchy affects lives across all social classes.

Cast:

Amir Karara, Mahmoud Abdel-Moghni, Mai Omar, Maged El Masry, Ahmed Khaled Saleh, Khaled El Sawy

Director:

Khaled Marei
