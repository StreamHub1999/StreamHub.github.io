Mufasa: The King of Tigers explores the origins of the iconic character from The Lion King and his rise to become the powerful ruler of the Pride Lands. The story follows Mufasa as a young, orphaned cub who forms an unlikely bond with his brother Taka (later Scar) as they find their way through a world where tigers govern the wilderness. Through trials and tribulations, audiences will discover how Mufasa found his place in the great circle of life and became the legendary leader we know.

Set in the stunning landscapes of the savanna with breathtaking visuals and inspiring new music, this film pays homage to the original classic while introducing a new generation to the beloved characters and expanding their story.
