Overview

After the collapse of the multiverse, Wade Wilson tackles depression by working as an ordinary car salesman. When the Time Variance Authority (TVA) recruits him for a crucial mission to save reality, Deadpool reluctantly teams up with the reclusive Wolverine, who is still recovering from his losses from the past. Together they embark on a journey across multiple dimensions, encountering both allies and enemies from various Marvel universes.

Bringing together two of Marvel's most iconic characters, this action-packed adventure combines Deadpool's irreverent humor with Wolverine's gritty intensity. As they traverse the multiverse, they must overcome their differences and confront their own demons to prevent the complete unraveling of reality. With stakes higher than ever before, this unlikely duo discovers that saving the world might require more than just their healing abilities and adamantium claws.
