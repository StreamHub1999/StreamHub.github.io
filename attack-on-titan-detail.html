Attack on Titan

2013-2023 ‚Ä¢ TV-MA ‚Ä¢ 4 Seasons ‚Ä¢ Anime, Action, Adventure, Drama, Fantasy

In a world where humanity lives within cities surrounded by enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their childhood friend Armin Arlert.

After a Colossal Titan breaches the wall of their home town, Eren vows revenge and to reclaim the world from the Titans, enlisting in the elite Survey Corps, a military unit that combats the Titans outside the walls. As the story progresses, they discover disturbing secrets about the Titans, their own civilization, and mysteries regarding Eren's ability to transform into a Titan himself, which could be key to humanity's survival.

Cast:

Yuki Kaji, Yui Ishikawa, Marina Inoue, Hiroshi Kamiya, Daisuke Ono, Romi Park

Director:
